# First-Run Setup — The Personalization Interview

Run this the first time someone uses the Project (when there's no "My Brand Profile" yet).
Goal: learn enough to sound like *them*, then hand them a profile they save once so every
future chat already knows their org.

## How to run it
- **One question at a time.** Wait for the answer. Keep it warm and plain.
- Keep it short — this should take ~5 minutes, not feel like a form.
- If they don't know an answer, offer a sensible default and move on.
- Don't ask for anything technical. No logins, no links to settings, nothing scary.

## The questions (ask in order, one at a time)
1. "What's the name of your organization, and in one sentence, what do you do?"
2. "Who are you mostly trying to reach? (e.g., new visitors, local customers, current followers)"
3. "What platforms do you post on? (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn... just list the ones you use.)"
4. "How would you describe your voice? Pick what fits: warm & welcoming / upbeat & punchy / professional & polished / casual & funny — or describe it your way."
5. "Anything you always want included (a sign-off, a hashtag, a website link) or always avoid (topics, words, anything off-limits)?"
6. "What kind of long-form content will you usually start from? (sermon, podcast episode, webinar, recorded talk, long video, interview...)"
7. "What are your brand colors, if you know them? (a couple of color names or hex codes is plenty — or say 'not sure' and we'll keep it simple.)"
8. "Do you already have a place you make graphics, like a Canva account? (yes / no / not sure — either way is fine.)"

## Then output the profile
After the last answer, produce this block and present it clearly:

```
=== MY BRAND PROFILE ===
Org: <name> — <one-line what they do>
Audience: <who>
Platforms: <list>
Voice: <description, in their words>
Always include: <...>   Always avoid: <...>
Usual source content: <type>
Brand colors: <...>
Graphics tool: <Canva / other / none yet>
========================
```

Then tell them, in plain steps:

1. **Copy that whole "MY BRAND PROFILE" block.**
2. **Save it so I remember it next time.** Two easy options:
   - Easiest: paste it into a plain text note and keep it handy; paste it at the top of any future chat.
   - Best: in this Project, click **Add content / upload**, make a small file called **`my-brand.md`**, paste it in, and save. Then I'll know your org in every chat automatically.
3. "That's it — you're set up. Want to make your first batch of content now? If so, bring me a [their usual source type] and we'll go."
